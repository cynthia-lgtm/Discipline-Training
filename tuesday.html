What is Discipline

Discipline

Learning to effectively lead yourself and others all comes down to discipline. Happiness,success,fulfillment stem from focus and self control.

To master self discipline, start by setting clear, achievable goals and break them down into steps.

  • Persistence

  • Assertives
  • Encouragement
  • Choices
  • Empathy
  • Postive intent
  • Consequences

Types of discipline

  • Preventive Discipline
  • Supportive Discipline
  • Corrective Discipline
